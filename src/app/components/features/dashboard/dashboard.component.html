Finance Management Application

Built by Tociva Private Limited

Accountant-first finance platform • Founder: Prince Francis

Daybook.Cloud helps accountants and small businesses manage GST data, bank statements, and journals with a clean, workflow-driven interface that feels natural for daily bookkeeping.

The Problem

  • SMEs and accountants spend hours manually posting invoices, journals, and bank entries.
  • GST data must be downloaded from the portal and then manually converted into invoices.
  • Bank statements require manual categorisation into receipts and payments.
  • Trading and inventory data needs to be re-entered to generate journals.
  • Existing tools are powerful but complex, requiring significant training.

Our Solution

Daybook.Cloud is built around simplicity and accountant-friendly workflows.

  • Convert GST-portal downloads into clean, usable invoices.
  • Upload bank statements and instantly create receipts & payments.
  • Use trading and inventory data to auto-generate journals.
  • Zero onboarding — auditors could operate the app without any training.

Traction

  • Actively used in real accounting workflows for two companies.
  • An external auditor operated the application without training — strong UX validation.
  • Real GST, bank, and journal data processed in daily use.
  • Early proof that the product is intuitive and ready for professional environments.

Current Feature Snapshot

  • Import data downloaded from the GST portal.
  • Monthly GST reconciliation: GSTR-2B vs GSTR-1.
  • Upload bank statements → create receipts & payments.
  • Create journals from trading and inventory data.
  • Generate invoices using custom invoice templates.
  • Multi-currency support for invoices and accounting.

Technology

  • Built using reliable open-source technologies to minimize development and infrastructure costs.
  • Architecture designed for scalability with minimal operational overhead.
  • Planned integration of our own open-source AI model for GST automation, bank categorization, and journal intelligence — ensuring zero token cost and full data privacy.
  • System supports offline-friendly and high-performance data workflows.

Business Model (Planned)

  • Accountant-friendly pricing for firms managing multiple companies.
  • Premium tiers once automation and AI-driven workflows are enabled.
